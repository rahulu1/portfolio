Rent-A-Bot

Rent-A-Bot is a chaotic co-op game where players control robots in a factory and collaborate to produce computer chips for their clients. The levels feature puzzling and engaging layouts that force the players to collaborate to meet their quotas on time!

My main work on the game focused primarily on the backend and UX development of the disasters and associated systems. I created the Disaster Queue, a system responsible for managing and randomly triggering events throughout each level in a flexible, customizable, and balanced manner. I also developed all the quick time events used to resolve the disasters, focusing primarily on making them intuitive, satisfying, and fun.
