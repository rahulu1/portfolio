TiltShift KnockOut

TiltShift KnockOut is a fast-paced party game where you and a friend compete to be the last one standing! Choose from a variety of cars, then enter the arena, crash, and dash your way to victory. Play as the agile HammerHead or the powerful SmackerJack. This game was the result of a 2-week rapid prototyping endeavor. My goal with this game was to quickly develop an incredibly powerful modular driving system, and then use that to create a game with arcadey, physics-based combat. There are two available gamemodes at the moment, and one vehicle class to choose from, with another in development.
